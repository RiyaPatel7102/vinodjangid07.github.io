<Products>

Ammonium Sulfate

Ammonium sulfate, with the chemical formula (NH4)2SO4, is an inorganic salt widely used as a fertilizer, particularly for alkaline soils. It's also employed in various industrial and laboratory applications, including protein purification and as a food additive.

Ammonium Persulfate

Ammonium persulfate, also known as APS, is a strong oxidizing agent and a powerful free radical generator used in various applications.

Potassium Persulfate

Potassium persulfate, also known as potassium peroxodisulfate or KPS, is an inorganic compound with the formula K2S2O8. It's a white, crystalline solid that is sparingly soluble in cold water but dissolves well in warm water.

Sodium Persulfate

Sodium persulfate, a white crystalline powder with the chemical formula Na2S2O8, is a strong oxidizing agent used in various industrial and commercial applications. It's primarily known for its use in polymerization processes, bleaching agents, and as a component in printed circuit board manufacturing.
